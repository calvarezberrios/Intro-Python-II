Welcome to the Ruins Adventures of Galeria
Here you will find instructions on how to
play and what you can do.

Make sure to press 'ENTER'/'RETURN' with a command

Actions                     Commands                    Description
Movement                    n, s, e, w                  Moves you to the 
                                                        room in the 
                                                        direction chosen  

Interact                    take/get "item name"        Interact with items
                            drop "item name"            in room or your 
                                                        inventory

View inventory              i, inventory                Show your inventory

Equip/Unequip Weapon        equip/unequip "item name"   Equip or unequip weapon 
                                                        to/from main hand

Quit Game                   q                           Quits the game
